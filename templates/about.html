About FudgeBuses

Welcome to FudgeBuses! My goal is to provide a reliable platform for users to report and track bad bus services that are consistently late or canceled. I understand how frustrating it can be to depend on public transport, especially when buses fail to arrive on time or are canceled without notice.

My Mission

My mission with FudgeBuses is to empower users by giving them a voice to report their experiences with bus services. By collecting and sharing these reports, I aim to create a transparent environment where users can make informed decisions about their travel options. This app is designed to:

  • Enable users to easily report late or canceled bus services.
  • Provide a historical overview of bus service performance in your area.
  • Allow users to view reports submitted by others, helping everyone stay informed.
  • Encourage bus companies to improve their services through community feedback.

Why Track Bus Services?

Public transportation is a vital part of many communities, but it can often be unreliable. Tracking bus services with FudgeBuses helps:

  • Identify patterns in service disruptions.
  • Improve accountability for bus companies.
  • Help commuters find alternative travel options when needed.
